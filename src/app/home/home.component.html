User friendly

Budgetam was built with the users in mind, we ensured that our app gives the user an intuitive experience. View your balance by a single tap on the switch and all transactions are categorized for easy tracking.

Data visualization

We know how important it is for our users to have an insight into their the cash in & out flow, so we provided a report generating feature that gives them the power to see their transaction history effortlessly.

About

This app was built to enable people who find it difficult to manage their income and expenses efficiently. We started by considering the question "What is the best way to keep track of transactions?". In other to solve this problem we created Budgetam, an app that will enable the user to track cash in & out flow with ease.
